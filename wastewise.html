Recyclable Materials

Material Recyclable? Recycling Time Description
Paper Yes 2-6 weeks Paper can be recycled multiple times. To recycle paper, collect clean and dry paper, remove any staples, and drop it off at a recycling center.
Cardboard Yes 2-4 weeks Cardboard is widely recyclable. Flatten boxes and remove any non-recyclable components (like plastic). Place in a recycling bin.
Plastic Bottles Yes 1-2 months Rinse bottles to remove residue. Remove caps and labels if required by your local program. Place in recycling bins for pickup.
Glass Bottles Yes 30 days Glass can be recycled indefinitely. Rinse bottles and jars, remove caps, and ensure they are clean before recycling.
Aluminum Cans Yes 2 months Rinse cans and remove labels if necessary. Cans can be recycled repeatedly without losing quality.
Styrofoam No N/A Styrofoam is not accepted in most recycling programs due to contamination issues. Dispose of it in the trash.
Food Waste Yes (Composting) Variable Food scraps can be composted to create nutrient-rich soil. Collect organic waste in a compost bin.
